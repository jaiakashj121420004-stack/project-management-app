Why Aurora

Your ideas don't live in one app. Now your work doesn't have to either.

A board for the plan. A doc for the thinking. A whiteboard for the messy middle. A calendar for the deadline. Aurora keeps them in one place, links them together, and never charges you to think.

Notes & docs

A document that builds itself as you type.

Press / and Aurora opens a menu of building blocks — headings, checklists, quotes, dividers, embeds, tables. No formatting toolbars to hunt through. Just write, and shape it in the same breath.

  • Notion-style block editor with a / slash command for everything
  • Rich text, checklists, callouts, code and cover images
  • Start blank or from a template you saved

Questions, answered.

Is Free actually free?

Yes — genuinely, forever. You get up to 10 boards, 3 collaborators per board, unlimited cards, notes, templates, folders, calendar, to-dos and browser reminders. No credit card, no trial clock.

What exactly do I get with Pro?

Unlimited boards and collaborators, the Notes Canvas (infinite collaborative whiteboard) with in-app recording, uploads and video/audio embeds, email due-date digests, custom timed reminders like “2 hours before”, and priority support.

How much is Pro?

$5.99/month, or $68.29/year (about $5.69/month — roughly 5% off). Prices are in USD and localized at checkout; taxes are handled for you.

Can I use Aurora on my phone?

Yes. Aurora installs as an app on both phone and desktop, launches full-screen, works offline, and syncs across all your devices.

Is my data private and secure?

Every workspace is protected by row-level security, so you only ever see your own data. Sign out on shared devices — your session and offline cache clear automatically.

Can I cancel anytime?

Anytime, in a click. You keep your data, and you simply drop back to the Free plan.
